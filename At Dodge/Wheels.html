Wheels of Dodge Charger 1969

Wheels

  • Standard Wheels: The 1969 Charger typically came with 14-inch wheels as standard equipment.
    These were often fitted with 6.5-inch wide steel wheels.

  • Optional Wheels: Larger wheel options were available, including 15-inch wheels, which were often paired with performance-oriented tires.
    These were generally 7 inches wide.
